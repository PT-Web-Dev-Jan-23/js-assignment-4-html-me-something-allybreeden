Nature From Around the World

California 🌊

"The mountains are calling and I must go" - John Muir

Finding myself on the West Coast was like being invited into a magical kingdom. From the tallest trees in the world to the epic superblooms in the Mojave desert, California is the place to call home. Like so many before me, I had an instant connection to the deep Pacific Ocean, calling mountains, and incredible wildlife. With so much to explore, I am sure that I will find myself there again soon.

Hawai'i 🌈

"Big Island, Small Island" -Hawaiian Locals

The Big Island of Hawai'i is often overlooked. It is preferred to escape to the bustling islands of Maui or O'ahu. Packed with 1 million dollar mansions and tourists, most visitors feel at ease. But for those with a taste for adventure, for solitude, & untouched nature - they seek out the Big Isalnd of Hawai'i. You will see chameleons basking in the afternoon sun, 'Òhi'a trees growing through pure lava rock, and secluded waterfalls waiting to be explored.

Cambodia 🌴

"The Place You Will Leave Smiling" -An Old Peace Corps Saying

There is nothing more beautiful than a country full of smiling people. Second to this, would be the breath taking nature of Cambodia. The Mekong River at dusk, the open fields of rice patties, and the red clay beneath your sandals are enough to make you fall in love with the country's landscapes.

Ireland 🐑

"The One Place You Will Never Want to Leave" -Myself

Ireland is not a place, it is a destination. The rolling hills of green, the breathtaking cliffsides, and the scent from the fields of fresh flowers fill the air. If you find yourself walking through the hills sides, you will have a hard time not getting lost in your sweet thoughts. Traveling through Ireland is like a nostalgic memory, waiting to be relived.

Now the question is... Where to go next?
